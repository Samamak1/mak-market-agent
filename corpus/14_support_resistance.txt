Support and Resistance Levels
Support and resistance are price zones where buying or selling interest has repeatedly appeared, visible as areas where price previously reversed or stalled. They form partly from memory and positioning: traders who bought at a level defend it, those who sold too early buy the retest, and stop orders cluster just beyond obvious levels. Round numbers, prior highs and lows, and high-volume zones all attract attention. A clean break of a level often flips its role, with old resistance acting as new support. Levels are zones rather than exact prices, and their usefulness comes from planning entries, exits and invalidation points in advance.
